## 1. ヒーロー画像

保存名: `hero-pancakes.jpg`

```text
Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: landing page hero image for a pancake cafe website
Primary request: a luxurious stack of fluffy Japanese soufflé pancakes served in a warm, elegant cafe
Scene/backdrop: cozy natural cafe interior with soft window light, wood table, subtle background blur
Subject: thick fluffy pancakes topped with whipped cream, berries, maple syrup, and powdered sugar
Style/medium: photorealistic food photography
Composition/framing: wide horizontal composition, pancakes placed slightly to the right, enough negative space on the left for website text
Lighting/mood: soft morning light, gentle, warm, inviting
Color palette: ivory, cream, soft pink beige, warm brown
Materials/textures: airy pancake texture, glossy syrup, soft whipped cream, ceramic plate, natural wood grain
Constraints: no people, no text, no watermark, premium cafe mood
Avoid: harsh shadows, cluttered background, unrealistic garnish, oversaturated colors
```
